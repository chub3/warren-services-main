Our Idea

Our Idea

Easy to Use Kerb-Side Car Charging

Our Idea is to introduce Kerb-Side charging to terraced streets, while remaining low profile, yet accessible

  • Low Profile

    Keeping a low profile, means you are still able to park regular cars without obstructions, or damage

  • Easy To Use

    With accessibility in mind, we have designed a functional yet simplistic to use design, while retaining its key features

  • Easy Installation

    With ease of installation in mind, we designed it such to be painless and user friendly, without requiring specialist tooling

European Standard

Large Compatibility

By using the EU standard mennekes plug, this ensures a wide range of compatibility, while also future-proofing our design

Fast Charging

Standard Fast Charging

All of our kerb side charges, are equipped with standard fast charging at 20-22kW, which slows down during the night, to prevent damage to battery and to reduce overall costs

App Mockup

App Prototype

Using an app to meter and provide accurate readings, current rates in the users area, users will be able to use Apple Pay, Google Pay or use a top-up system to pay.
